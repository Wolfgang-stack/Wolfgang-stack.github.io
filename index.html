    • Giorgia McRae is an emerging artist based in Sydney, Australia. Through her practice, she explores both sculpture and drawing and the playful relationship between the two mediums. McRae's sculptures investigate the concept of drawing in space with steel, while her drawings focus on line, shape and colour. McRae has just completed a Bachelor of Fine Arts at the National Art School.
